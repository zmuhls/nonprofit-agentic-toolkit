Non-Profit AI Toolkit
A guided decision process for nonprofit staff

Decide whether AI belongs in your organization.

A guided learning and decision process for nonprofit staff.

The toolkit explains the AI concepts you need, asks one question at a time, and builds an AI Adoption Record from your answers. Your organization stays responsible for every decision.

Each test leads to Proceed, Negotiate and return, or Walk Away.

How the process works

Begin with the proposed use and its connection to your mission. The record then carries your answers, conditions, owners, and unknowns through six tests.
